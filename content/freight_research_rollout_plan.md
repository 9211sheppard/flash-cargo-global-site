# Freight Research Rollout Plan

Goal: build a premium, source-backed Freight Research library that answers shipment questions before the reader is ready to contact a freight company.

Scope:
- 68 approved topics
- 5 languages: English, Spanish, French, Chinese, Hindi
- 340 future pages total if every topic is localized

Publishing order:
1. Country and lane pages
2. Industry and product pages
3. Document pages
4. Agency and compliance pages
5. Freight decision pages
6. Receiver problem pages
7. Risk and delay pages

Rules:
- Match the site story: North America base, global air/ocean lanes, North American trucking/customs/warehousing/white glove.
- Use official source URLs per lane or topic.
- Do not stuff every page with every source.
- Do not publish raw email or phone.
- Do not use internal strategy wording on public pages.
