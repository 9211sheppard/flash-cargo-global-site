Vietnam footwear manufacturer to European retailer export checklist

What footwear factories should organize before shipping seasonal shoes, samples, or replenishment orders to Europe.

Footwear shipments combine apparel-like buyer requirements with product-specific packing and labeling details. Before booking, the factory should confirm style references, size runs, carton count, pair count, material description, country of origin, buyer routing guide, delivery window, and whether the order supports samples, seasonal launch, retail replenishment, or returns.

Europe-bound shipments should be checked against buyer instructions and destination expectations. If labels, carton-level data, delivery appointments, or staged release matter, the freight plan should be built before the cargo leaves the factory.
