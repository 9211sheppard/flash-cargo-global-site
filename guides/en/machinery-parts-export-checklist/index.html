Machinery and industrial parts

Machinery and industrial parts export checklist

A pre-shipment guide for equipment, spare parts, tooling, and industrial components moving by air, ocean, or road.

Use this checklist before you book freight.

  • Confirm dimensions, weight, lifting points, crate strength, and whether cargo needs forklift, crane, or flatbed handling.
  • Match serial numbers, part numbers, country of origin, and invoice descriptions so customs and receiver records agree.
  • Plan insurance, inspection photos, and delivery equipment before the shipment is booked.

Official sources

  • International Trade Administration - Know Your Incoterms
  • World Customs Organization - Harmonized System
