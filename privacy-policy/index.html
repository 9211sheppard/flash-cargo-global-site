Privacy Policy

Flash Cargo Global uses contact form information to review and respond to freight inquiries.

Information handled

  • Contact details submitted through the site form, such as name, company, email, phone if provided, and freight needs.
  • Shipment context voluntarily provided by the sender, such as route, cargo type, timing, and documentation needs.
  • Basic technical form protections used to reduce automated spam and protect the inquiry process.

Flash Cargo Global does not publish contact form submissions on the website.
