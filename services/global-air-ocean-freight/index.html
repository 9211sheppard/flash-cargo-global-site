Global air and ocean freight

Air and ocean freight planning for cargo that needs the right balance of speed, cost, routing, documentation, and handling control.

Air versus ocean decision points

  • Use air when time risk, launch dates, production downtime, or buyer commitments matter more than cost per kilo.
  • Use ocean when shipment size, cost control, and inventory planning allow longer transit and port handling.
  • Check documents, HS code context, cargo value, Incoterms, and destination clearance requirements before booking.
