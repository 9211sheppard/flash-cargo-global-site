Customs support

North American customs support

Customs support starts before cargo moves: cleaner documents reduce confusion at the border, at the terminal, and with the receiver.

Documents to prepare early

  • Commercial invoice with buyer, seller, value, currency, Incoterms, and clear goods descriptions.
  • Packing list with package count, dimensions, weights, and marks that match the physical shipment.
  • HS code context, country of origin, broker details, and any certificate or label requirements tied to the goods.
