Careful handling

Warehousing and white glove service

Warehousing and white glove freight support helps when cargo needs staging, careful handling, timed delivery, or receiver-sensitive execution.

When careful handling matters

  • Use warehousing when freight needs short-term storage, consolidation, inspection, relabeling, or staged release.
  • Use white glove support when the delivery environment, cargo value, receiver expectations, or handling risk requires extra planning.
  • Confirm access limits, packaging protection, appointment timing, and proof-of-condition expectations before pickup.
